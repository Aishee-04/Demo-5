FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The 2024 FIFA Futsal World Cup was the tenth edition of the FIFA Futsal World Cup,the futsal championship contested by the men's national teams of the member associations of FIFA.

It was the first FIFA tournament ever hosted by Uzbekistan and a Central Asian country. The tournament returns to its usual four-year cycle after the previous tournament was delayed for a year.

Portugal were the defending champions by defeating Argentina in the previous final 2–1, but were eliminated in the round of 16 against Kazakhstan. Brazil won a record-extending sixth title.

Destinations

  • Held in Dubai, UAE.
  • Took place from 15–25 February 2024.
  • Organized by FIFA.
  • Played at Dubai Design District Stadium.
  • 16 teams participated.
  • Divided into 4 groups.
  • Top 2 teams from each group advanced.
  • Brazil won the tournament.
  • Italy was the runner-up.
  • Mauricinho (Brazil) was top scorer.
  • It was UAE’s first time hosting.
  • Brazil claimed their 6th title.
